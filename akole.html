Akole "A heven of beauty"

Akole is a city and Taluka in Akole tehsil, Ahmednagar district in the state of Maharashtra, India. Akole is surrounded by the Sahyadri Mountains of Western Ghats, India. Several historical sites in the city honor Akole's connection to the history and culture of Maharashtra.

Hisory
17-7-23

It is believed that lord Rama visited Akole after departing to 14 years Vanvaas. King Harishchandra built a fort for his wife Taramati known as Harishchandragad it is located in Akole tehsil. Great Maratha emperor Shivaji Maharaj visited Vishramgad in Akole after looting Surat and halted here for 30 days to recover from the exhaustion due to the Surat tour.

Historical sites

17-7-23

Agasti Rishi Ashram is situated on the banks of the Pravara River. As mentioned in the Ramayana, it is believed that Lord Ram, Lakshman and Sita visited Sage Agastya here, where the Rishi then offered Lord Ram a miraculous arrow which he later used to kill Ravan. Amruteshwar Temple is located at the entrance of the Ratanwadi village. It is considered to be over 1,200 years old and is dedicated to Lord Shiva as the main deity.
