Fireflies Festival

Fireflies are called Kajava or Kajva in Marathi, Kajwa Mahotsav is a firefly camping experience out of the world, and strangers are just friends you haven't met yet at Kajwa Mahotsav Fireflies put on a dazzling light show after sundown, and they arent only for show. They communicate by flickering their lights -notably during courtship rituals.
