Randha Fall

At a distance of 3 km to the north from Bhandardara , the Pravara river flows into a 50 meter deep valley. That place is called Randha Falls. Here is the temple of Ghorpada Devi. Randha Falls is the invention of a roaring youth. Born in the Kushi of Ratangadda, the Pravara river, aftertaking a 20 km walk through the mountain range, suddenly descends into a 50 meter deep valley.At that time, there was a musical atmosphere created by the combination of all that rainy mangakallol of water, the white smoke of the water, the majesty of the frost and the rainbow rising onthose frosts, the sound of the deafening silence. In the rainy season, the rainbow falls even more dramatically. But at the same time, there are dangerous situations. In the rainy season, the waterfall at Katalpur on the other side is noticeable but immediately after the falls. Many Bollywood film directors also fell in love with RandhaFalls. There fore, the thrilling scenes of many other hit films like Maine Pyar Kiya, Prem, Kurban and Raju Chacha are featured here.
Randha water fall in winter:- Randha water fall In winter, water flow is less but it gave us a advantage to us saw different view of Randha Waterfall. An unbelievable capacity of Pravara river. Due to less water, we saw a deep downfall which some were around 200 feet. Waterfall in three steps. Which sounds very nice. Riverbank is made of a full big rock. As we are on top. we saw a similar height as the big rock mountain. Riverbank was distributed in two parts. One goes straight and another turns left side. Because of this type scenic view point of Randha waterfall has become the best tourist attraction in winter.
